Crop Protection From Animals

Alert System for Crop Protection

India ranks second worldwide in farm outputs. Indian agriculture playes a major role in food plant and medicinal plant production. But there is high risk of animals attacking the field. In an year, approximately 20,000 hectars of crop damage is caused by animals. To avoid this scenario, this system helps you monitoring the farm in your absence and alerts on any kind of animal detected.
